Super Admin · structure & hierarchy

The single desktop screen in the app. OSD opens this on a laptop to configure who's who, who reports to whom, and where each person sits in the structure.

Try these

  • Drag the two Dy. Secretaries to reorder them under the Director
  • Drag the two Section Officers under Pooja D. to reorder them
  • Drag any unassigned officer up into the chain (or vice versa)
  • Click any officer card — inspector updates on the right
  • Tap "Add user" — modal with hierarchy slot, designation, division
  • Tap "New" next to Divisions in the sidebar
  • Tap "Command Centre" in the role switcher — flips back to OSD mode

Design notes

  • Hierarchy slot vs designation — the "(YP)" / "(PO)" badge shows contract roles; the slot underneath them is what governs permissions
  • Indigo throughout — Super Admin uses indigo where the OSD/JS side uses amber
  • Officer cards lighten as you go down the slot ladder — subtle visual depth cue
  • Selected card gets an indigo ring; the inspector mirrors the selection
